About

Author: Tim Mazyrko

Tim Mazyrko (more officially known as Timothy) is a citizen of 20 years, and a netizen of nearly just as long. Born on the cusp of Y2K, he grew up surrounded by computers, technology, and videogames, in large part thanks to his father's IT profession and his brother's early 2000s Lineage II addiction. Tim is currently a student at the New Jersey Institute of Technology, and studies IT with a concentration in Network and Information Security. He is an avid music lover on the side, and enjoys both consuming and creating it in various forms. He also enjoys playing videogames, and sometimes streams himself doing exactly that on Twitch. Last but not least, he is proficient in a variety of languages including Java, Python, HTTP/CSS, JavaScript, PHP, English, and Russian. If you'd like to contact Tim with a job offer or check out his credentials, head over to his LinkedIn profile from the Contact page.
